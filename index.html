Driving Tips

Speed Cameras

Victoria has zero-tolerance enforcement. Cameras trigger even 3km/h over. Stick strictly to signed limits.

Look Right First: When entering roads, traffic approaches from your right. Mirror your instincts.

Roundabouts: Travel clockwise. Give way to vehicles already in the circle (approaching from your right).

No Left on Red: Illegal unless a sign specifically allows it. Always wait for the green light.

Wildlife: Kangaroos are a major risk at dusk. If you see one, assume others are nearby.

Aussie Culture & Dining

The "Parmi"

Standard pub meal (Chicken Parmigiana) served with chips and salad. Must be called a "Parmi."

Beer Sizes

Pot: 10oz | Schooner: 15oz | Pint: 20oz. Victoria favors Pots and Pints.

Coffee

Order a Flat White (velvety milk) or Long Black (Americano style). No "regular" drip coffee.

Tipping

Not expected. Tax (GST) is included in menu prices. The price you see is what you pay.

Key Game Terms

● The Mark: Catch the ball from a kick (>15m) and get a free, unhindered kick.

● Handball: No throwing! Ball must be punched off the palm of the other hand.

● Holding the Ball: Tackled? You must release the ball immediately. Don't "prioritize" it.

● 50m Penalty: If a player slows down a free kick, the umpire moves the ball 50m forward.

Fan Lingo

When an umpire misses a tackle call, the crowd yells "BAAALLL!" at the top of their lungs.
