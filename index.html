4.0 Packing & Weather

"Four Seasons in One Day"

Victoria’s climate is volatile. Expect 15-degree drops in under an hour. Be prepared for sun, rain, and a sharp "Cool Change" in a single afternoon.

6.0 Road Rules & Safety

Speed Cameras

Victoria has zero-tolerance enforcement. Stick strictly to signed limits.

!

Look Right First: Traffic approaches from your right at every intersection.

!

Roundabouts: Travel clockwise. Give way to vehicles already in the circle.

!

No Left on Red: Illegal unless a sign specifically allows it.

!

Wildlife: Kangaroos are a major risk at dusk. Stop if one is in your path.
